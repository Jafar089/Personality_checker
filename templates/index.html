Personality Test

Are you an extrovert or an introvert? Extroverts are often talkative and enjoy social gatherings, while introverts prefer solitary activities and require less social interaction.

Are you sensing or intuitive? Sensing individuals are practical, prefer routine, and focus on details. Intuitive individuals think about possibilities, innovate, and focus on the big picture.

Are you thinking or feeling? Thinking individuals make decisions based on logic and reason, often detached from emotions. Feeling individuals prioritize emotions and the impact of decisions on others.

Are you judging or perceiving? Judging individuals prefer order and structure in their lives. Perceiving individuals are more flexible and open to changes and spontaneity.
